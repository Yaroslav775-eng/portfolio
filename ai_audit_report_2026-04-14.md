## 4. Strategic Recommendations
### Priority 1: Floating "Quick Connect" CTA
**Action:** Add a subtle, fixed-position button or link in the bottom right corner (e.g., a "Hire Me" or "Telegram" icon).
**Rationale:** Ensure users can reach out the moment they are impressed by a specific case study without scrolling to the end.

### Priority 2: Case Study Deep Links
**Action:** Once you have 100+ visitors, check which case gets the most clicks. Move that case to the #1 position.
**Rationale:** Lead with your highest-converting "winner" case based on real data.

### Priority 3: Sticky Header for Navigation
**Action:** Make the `<header>` or a simplified navigation bar sticky.
**Rationale:** Keep the "Open CV" and "Contact" goals always accessible while the user is reading long-form case descriptions.
