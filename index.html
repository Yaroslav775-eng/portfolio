Pet Supplies: Structuring for Margin Scale

Resolving algorithmic cannibalization and intent-overlap to unlock true profitability.

Strategic Insight: The account suffered from profound intent mixing. High-converting Branded queries and low-intent Generic traffic were colliding within the same structures, muddying the machine learning algorithms and artificially inflating ROAS while actively masking wasted spend on generic terms.

The Challenge

A national e-commerce player needed to break through a strict scaling plateau. Every attempt to increase daily budget resulted in rapid CPA degradation. The root cause was a chaotic, monolithic account architecture where the algorithm lacked strict constraint inputs, leading it to bid on whatever was easiest rather than what actually moved the bottom line.

Surgical Execution

  • Intent Isolation: Segmented campaigns rigorously by intent tier, essentially forcing search terms into correct funnels via cross-pollinating negative keyword lists.
  • Feed Intelligence: Rebuilt the Google Merchant Center (GMC) mapping so bidding algorithms could be assigned distinct ROAS targets based on actual unit economics, not just gross top-line revenue.
  • Budget De-risking: Shifted the massive budget pipeline entirely to priority SKUs with proven margin viability, systematically starving legacy campaigns of ad-spend.

Business Impact

We developed a transparent, highly-controlled account architecture. Priority segments achieved unprecedented stability, allowing founders to predict exact net-margin returns for every €1,000 injected into the pipeline.

Logistics Enterprise: Search Filtration

Engineering high-intent architectures to filter out consumer "junk" traffic.

Strategic Insight: Google's automated networks continuously conflate complex B2B service queries with basic B2C consumer queries. We had to physically rip out the automation to establish a heavy, manual Search framework that protected the budget.

The Challenge

A B2B logistics enterprise was suffering from severe lead-quality degradation. Although the Google Ads interface showed 'cheap leads', the sales team was overwhelmed by a high volume of disqualified, low-value consumer inquiries that wasted hours of representative time.

Surgical Execution

  • Broad Match Elimination: Paused overly broad automated structures and rebuilt exact/phrase architectures for strict query containment.
  • Massive B2C Exclusion Lists: Engineered a master library of over 1,500 negative keywords specifically designed to sever informational/consumer tracking queries from commercial B2B contract queries.
  • Value-Based Focus: Redirected 80% of the freed-up budget into bottom-funnel Search terms targeting enterprise logistics managers.

Business Impact

Lead quality dramatically reversed course. The sales pipeline was cleared of consumer noise, resulting in highly precise CPL tracking and a massive increase in actual signed contracts per ad dollar spent.

E-commerce 10k+ SKUs Margin Engineering

Electronics: Margin Distribution Control

Aligning advertising behavior directly to the CFO's profit margins.

Strategic Insight: Relying on blanket "Maximize Conversion Value" bidding across an inventory of 10,000+ mixed-margin SKUs is dangerous. We had to break Google's aggregation and connect spend behavior directly to warehouse margins.

The Challenge

A massive electronics retailer was actively burning budget. Google Ads was driving massive volume, but it was primarily pushing cheap, low-margin accessories (cables, adapters) while ignoring high-margin flagship hardware, destroying the blended margin.

Surgical Execution

  • Dismantling Automation: Completely deleted the existing 'Catch-All' smart shopping/PMax campaigns that were pooling logic.
  • Custom Labeling: Integrated with the backend API to inject precise Custom Labels (High Margin, Low Margin, Clearance) directly into the feed.
  • Staggered Bidding: Deployed specific targeted sub-campaigns featuring aggressively staggered ROAS limits, ensuring expensive clicks were only bought for items with the headroom to support them.

Business Impact

Total transparency was successfully established across the catalog. The 650% ROAS achieved functionally represented true profit, as ad-spend distribution was finally controlled by absolute margin priorities rather than algorithmic laziness.
