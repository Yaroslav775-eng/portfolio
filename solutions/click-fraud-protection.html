The Mechanics of Click Fraud

If you're in a competitive niche like home services, law, or B2B software, your competitors have every incentive to "click you away." It’s highly effective, cheap for them, and disastrous for you. Most marketers hope the Google Ads internal detection catches it. The reality? Google only catches the most obvious bot farms, leaving you to pay for the sophisticated stuff.

Our methodology shifts from passive observation to active perimeter defense.
